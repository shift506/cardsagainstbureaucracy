# Provocations Cards

**Suit:** Provocations
**Persona:** The Philosopher
**Total cards:** 19
**SVG source:** `Provocations/`

These cards anchor the Philosopher's contributions. Each card offers a provocation — a question, a paradox, or a challenge to a shared assumption — that opens new territory. When the Philosopher draws a Provocations card, they use it to reframe how the challenge is being understood and invite the group to sit with it.
