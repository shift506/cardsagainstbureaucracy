## Logo

### Primary Logo

The logo's waveform graphic gives the impression of an expansive, malleable mesh that can conform to any shape. It speaks to both Shiftflow's ability to tackle any problem, no matter the size or shape, as well as the belief in clients' ability to adapt and transform through the process.

The wordmark offers a subtle hint at transformation, as it begins in one typeface and slowly shifts into another. The hollow dot in the 'i' marks a moment of realization, and the beginning of the shift in letterforms.

---

### Secondary Logos

Secondary logos are available in the following background contexts:
- White background (light mode)
- New Leaf (`#D6DE23`) background
- Galaxy (`#10213C`) background

---

### Logo Form Variations

| Variation | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Landscape** | Symbol + wordmark side by side (horizontal) |
| **Stacked** | Symbol above wordmark (vertical) |
| **Wordmark** | Text-only version |
| **Symbol** | Icon/mark only |

---

### Logo Colour Variations

Each logo form variation is available in each of the logo colour variations. For guidance on when to use which colour variation, refer to the [Colour System](#colour-system) section.

Available colour variations include:
- Full colour on Galaxy (dark) background
- Full colour on white background
- New Leaf / Galaxy two-tone
- Monochrome (Galaxy only)
- Breeze tint
- Ocean tint
- Blossom tint

---

### Logo Misuse

The logo is the element viewed most by the audience and the element over which we have the least control. Every effort must be made to deliver the logo consistently every time it is displayed.

**DO NOT:**

- ❌ Stretch or distort the logo in any way
- ❌ Reposition any part of the logo
- ❌ Use the logo in any colour other than those listed in the guidelines
- ❌ Use a colour logo over top of complex patterns or photography
- ❌ Rotate the logo or symbol
- ❌ Use a colour logo overtop of a New Leaf, Breeze, or Blossom background
- ❌ Alter the proportions of the logo
- ❌ Change the wordmark

### Colour Story

Colour evokes an emotional response in its viewer. Using culture- and psychology-based marketing data, colour is used to elicit particular feelings and impact the way the business is perceived. The collective tale the colour palette choices tell is called the colour story.

| Colour | Emotional Qualities |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Galaxy** | Thoughtful, professional, trustworthy |
| **New Leaf** | Fresh, energetic, invigorating |
| **White** | Clean, bright, modern, spacious |
| **Breeze** | Tranquil, airy |
| **Ocean** | Deep |
| **Blossom** | Vibrant, creative |

---

### Colour Ratio

| Colour | Role | Usage |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Galaxy** | Primary | 40% |
| **New Leaf** | Secondary | 25% |
| **White** | Neutral | 20% |
| **Breeze** | Accent | 5% |
| **Ocean** | Accent | 5% |
| **Blossom** | Accent | 5% |

#### Primary Colour
**Galaxy** is the colour most strongly associated with the Shiftflow brand and should be used most often across branded materials.

#### Secondary Colour
**New Leaf** is the secondary colour of the brand and should be used approximately half as much as the primary colour and twice as much as the accent colours.

#### Neutral Colour
**White** is the chosen neutral colour to provide high contrast with other colours and to allow rest for the viewers' eyes between design elements.

#### Accent Colours
**Breeze**, **Ocean**, and **Blossom** add the emotional content that completes the colour story. They should be limited and used strategically for eye-catching elements such as buttons and links.

---

### Colour Usage

The following background/foreground pairings are approved:

- **Galaxy background** — White, New Leaf, or gradient text/elements
- **White background** — Galaxy, New Leaf, or Ocean text/elements
- **New Leaf background** — Galaxy text/elements

---

### Colour Misuse

**DO NOT use the following colour combinations:**

- ❌ Black font on **any** background
- ❌ Breeze font on New Leaf background
- ❌ Blossom font on New Leaf background
- ❌ New Leaf font on Breeze background
- ❌ Blossom font on Breeze background
- ❌ Blossom font on Ocean background
- ❌ New Leaf font on Blossom background
- ❌ Breeze font on Blossom background

> **Note:** The colour black should be avoided completely in all branded material. Instead of black, use **Galaxy** — including for fonts, shapes, and backgrounds.

## Typography

### Typefaces

#### Titles & Headings — Poppins Medium
- Style: Legible | Open | Optimistic | Approachable
- Weight: Medium
- Use for: All headings and display titles

#### Subheadings — Glacial Indifference
- Style: Modern | Humanist | Professional
- Use for: Section labels, subheadings, and supporting hierarchy

#### Body Copy — Poppins (Regular)
- Style: Legible | Open | Approachable
- Use for: All paragraph text and general body content
